3. When do you think AI is most useful for coding?

I think AI is most useful for coding when it comes to debugging errors in the code. By using AI to be able to analyze errors in my code, AI not only analyzes them to pinpoint the exact error but also suggest possible fixes to them as well. Furthermore, while AI is great for debugging my code, it's still important for me to be able to understand why the bug occurred and how to prevent it from occuring again. In addition, AI is also the most useful when it comes to trying to understand a difficult concept that needs further explanation. If I start to utilize unfamiliar languages or libraries, AI can be used in order to speed up the learning process to familiarize said languages and libraries.
